对emacs进行配置

  1. 更新插件包源:

    (require 'package)

    (setq package-enable-at-startup nil)

    (setq package-archives '(("melpa" . "https://melpa.org/packages/")))

    (package-initialize)

  2. 设置行号:

    (global-linum-mode t)

  3. 设置ido模式,打开文件时列出当前目录下文件名:

    (require 'ido)

    (ido-mode t)

  4. 安装helm插件:
  5. 设置smex模式,打开命令时列出候选命令名:

    (smex-initialize)

    (global-set-key (kbd "M-x") 'smex)

    (global-set-key (kbd "M-X") 'smex-major-mode-commands)

    ;; This is your old M-x.

    (global-set-key (kbd "C-c C-c M-x") 'execute-extended-command)

  6. 设置tab缩进:

    (defun my-custom-settings-fn () (setq indent-tabs-mode t) (setq tab-stop-list (number-sequence 2 200 2)) (setq tab-width 2) (setq indent-line-function 'insert-tab)) (add-hook 'text-mode-hook 'my-custom-settings-fn)

  7. 设置高亮:

    install auto-highlight hl-angything

    (require 'auto-highlight-symbol) (global-auto-highlight-symbol-mode t) (global-hl-line-mode t)

  8. 设置括号高亮:

    install lighhight-parentheses

    (require 'highlight-parentheses)

    (global-highlight-parentheses-mode t)

  9. 设计主题：

  10. 安装company插件，设置自动补全：

    (add-hook 'after-init-hook 'global-company-mode)

  11. 设置company-web-html:

    (require 'company-web-html)

    (global-set-key (kbd "C-'") 'company-web-html)

  12. 导入目录:

    (add-to-list 'load-path "diretory")

  13. 安装emmet插件,补全标签:

    (global-set-key (kbd "C-j") 'emmet-mode)

  14. 安装高亮缩进插件:

    (add-hook 'prog-mode-hook 'highlight-indent-guides-mode)

    (setq highlight-indent-guides-method 'character)

    (setq highlight-indent-guides-character ?\|)
